Our Mission

LunguageScore advances medical AI evaluation with innovative and clinically relevant assessment. Accurate evaluation of AI-generated medical reports is critical for safe and effective deployment in care settings.

What we do

  • Assess AI-generated medical reports with clinical accuracy
  • Provide comprehensive metrics for research and development
  • Support longitudinal analysis for patient-level trajectories
  • Enable reproducible research in medical AI

Our approach

Semantic understanding

Transformer-based entity and relation modeling captures clinical meaning and context beyond string matching.

Multi-modal evaluation

  • Semantic similarity — meaning-based entity matching
  • Temporal consistency — longitudinal change analysis
  • Structural alignment — report organization assessment

Clinical validation

All components are validated against expert assessments for real-world applicability.

LUNGUAGE dataset

A radiologist-annotated benchmark of structured chest X-ray reports derived from the MIMIC-CXR test split. It supports both single-report interpretation and longitudinal reasoning.

Composition

  • 1,473 reports from 230 patients
  • 17,000+ entities
  • 23,000+ relation-attribute pairs across 18 relation types

Longitudinal subset

  • 80 sequential reports from 10 patients
  • 3 to 14 studies per patient
  • Time gaps 1 to 1,200 days
  • 41,000+ pairwise comparisons

Schema and vocabulary

Schema-aligned vocabulary over diagnostic entities and attributes. All annotations verified by board-certified radiologists.

LunguageScore metric

A two-stage evaluation that first structures free-text into schema-aligned entities and relations, then computes a joint score that reflects clinical faithfulness.

Semantic score

Meaning-aware matching between ground truth and prediction at the entity level.

Structural score

Alignment of attributes, relations, and report organization.

Temporal score

Consistency across time with patient-level trajectory analysis.
